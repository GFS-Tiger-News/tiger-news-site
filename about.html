About the club

Our mission:

Every month, the Tiger Newscast club creates a newscast summarizing interesting goings on at Germantown Friends School. We meet every Tuesday, and generally release our videos in the beginning of each school month. Our club does not profit from Tiger News videos, nor do we plan on it.
When: Tuesdays at lunch
Where: HC-202
